About Us

Stressed? We get it.

The college admissions process is not easy, and its not supposed to be. You’re taking the first steps toward the next four or five years of your life, a journey that begins with a single word.

While you can’t go back and change the last four years of your life, your essays are something personal to you, and with the added uncertainties of a global pandemic, being focused is becoming more important now than ever.

You can’t choose your home or ever truly eliminate the distractions inherent to your environment, but you can clean up your online essay writing ecosystem.

Happlicant offers a sleek and centralized solution to the distraction problem, offering a focused environment to brainstorm, write, and research.
